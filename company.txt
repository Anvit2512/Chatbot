# About AquaBot Innovations

Founded in 2022, AquaBot Innovations is a tech startup dedicated to improving public health through smart hydration solutions. Our flagship product, the AquaBot X1000, was launched in early 2023.

Our mission is to make hydration effortless and intelligent. We are a team of engineers, designers, and health enthusiasts based in San Francisco, California. We believe that technology can be a powerful ally in building healthy habits.
